Welcome to Settlers of Cambridge: A battle for control of university row.
Player one plays as the fanny-pack loving hipsters, fighting for culture and unbridled expression, while player two plays as the briefcase-toting biotech businessmen who build the cities financial backbone.


The goal of the game is to get 5 victory points.
You acquire victory points by claiming spaces on the map.
Each space is worth 1 victory point and costs 2 dollars to build.
Every time a number is rolled, the player who owns that space gets a dollar.
